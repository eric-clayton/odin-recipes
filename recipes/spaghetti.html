Spaghetti

Basic but delicious classic spaghetti recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound thin spaghetti
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Boil 3 cups of water salt in a large pot and add salt.
  2. While water is boiling, in a separate pot heat the sauce on medium.
  3. When the water starts to boil, add the spaghetti and stir until submerged. Stir occasionally and cook for 6 minutes.
  4. Drain spaghetti in a strainer then combine the spaghetti and marinara sauce in a bowl and serve.
