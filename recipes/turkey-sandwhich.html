Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Nothing beats a simple, deli turkey sandwich. Try this easy 5-minute sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 4 ounces sliced or shaved deli turkey
  • 2 slices Provolone cheese (or your favorite sliced cheese)
  • Green leaf lettuce
  • 2 slices fresh tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Lay out all ingredients.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on bread slices and layer one slice with sliced turkey, Swiss cheese, lettuce, tomato, salt and pepper, and any other additional toppings.
  3. Slices of bread with turkey and cheese next to tomatoes and lettuce
  4. Top sandwich with remaining bread slice, cut into halves, and serve.
